About Me

Hello!

I'm Connor, a passionate and experienced software developer with a strong background in designing and implementing scalable, high-performance software solutions. Over the years, I have honed my skills at two of the tech industry's most respected companies: Intel and Amazon.

At Intel, I worked on cutting-edge projects that pushed the boundaries of what's possible with hardware and software integration. My role involved developing low-level system software and optimizing performance for various Intel hardware platforms. This experience gave me a deep understanding of computer architecture and the intricacies of high-performance computing.

Transitioning to Amazon, I embraced the challenge of building and scaling cloud-based applications and services. Here, I contributed to projects that impact millions of users worldwide. My time at Amazon allowed me to dive deep into cloud technologies, distributed systems, and microservices architecture. I was part of a dynamic team that thrived on innovation and rapid iteration, constantly striving to enhance user experience and operational efficiency.

In addition to my technical expertise, I am a firm believer in continuous learning and collaboration. I enjoy staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technologies and often participate in tech meetups and conferences. I'm also passionate about mentoring junior developers and sharing my knowledge through code reviews, pair programming, and technical blogs.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring the great outdoors, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or indulging in a good book.

Let's connect and build something amazing together!
